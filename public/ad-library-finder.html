Ad Library Finder FAQ

Is this an ad spy tool?

This is an ad research launcher for public transparency libraries. It does not bypass platform privacy controls.

Do all libraries show the same ad data?

No. Each library has different filters, retention windows, and visibility rules depending on region and policy.

What should I search first?

Start with brand keywords, then test domains and offer phrases to map a full competitive ad landscape.
