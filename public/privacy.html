Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

YouTube Promo Finder is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or track personal information about our users.

  • No Account Required: You can use our service without creating an account or providing personal information.
  • No Cookies: We do not use cookies to track your behavior.
  • YouTube Data: We only query publicly available YouTube data through the YouTube Data API v3.
  • Server Logs: Standard server logs may temporarily record IP addresses for technical troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Information

When you enter a YouTube channel URL:

  • We query the YouTube Data API v3 to retrieve publicly available video information
  • We temporarily cache results to improve performance and reduce API calls
  • We analyze video descriptions to identify product links and promotions
  • No personal data is stored permanently

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • YouTube Data API v3: To retrieve channel and video information. Subject to YouTube's Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: For hosting. Subject to Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN: For styling.

4. Data Retention

Cached search results are stored in memory for up to 15 minutes and are automatically deleted. We do not maintain long-term databases of user queries or results.

5. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal information, there's no data to access, correct, or delete. Your searches are ephemeral and not tied to your identity.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are under 13, please use this service with parental supervision.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through GitHub.
