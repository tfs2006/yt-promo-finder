Frequently Asked Questions

What can this tool actually find?

This tool finds unlisted YouTube videos that appear inside public playlists on a channel. If a creator has placed an unlisted video into a public playlist, the scanner can surface it with the title, thumbnail, publish date, and watch link.

Why are some unlisted videos missing?

Some unlisted videos are missing because they were never placed in a public playlist, or the relevant playlist is private. This tool only finds videos that are discoverable through public playlist coverage.

What inputs does the scanner support?

You can paste a YouTube channel URL, an @handle, or a channel ID. The tool resolves the channel and checks its public playlists for discoverable unlisted videos.

Is the unlisted video finder completely free?

Yes, 100% free. No account creation, no email signup, and no credit card. Just paste the channel URL and search.

Does this tool bypass privacy?

No. We only use public YouTube data and public playlists. The scanner does not brute-force video IDs, access private videos, or work around YouTube privacy settings.
